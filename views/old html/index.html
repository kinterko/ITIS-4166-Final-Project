Adventurers! We welcome you to The Unlikely Heroes!



If you are new to Dungeons & Dragons or other Table Top Role Playing Games (TTRPGs) this club is the perfect starting point!

Our website holds many different pages to help you along the way of becoming a part of the Unlikely Heroes that play TTRPGs.

Our "Start a new connection" page will let you create your own event to host others for game nights.

Our "Sign-up" page will help you get registered for certain events that may interest you and save your information for later use.

Our "Login" page can be used to save your information and connections. A faster and more convenient way to be a part of the Unlikely Heroes!
